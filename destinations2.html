Surfers Paradise, Australia

Surfing and bungee jumping can be best done here.

The beach and the surfing lifestyle are embedded not only in the Australian way of life, but in Australians’ hearts as well, and it’s no surprise why. Offering an abundance of beaches that not only are suitable for sunbathing but also produce some of the best surf waves in the world, these are the beaches you must head to if you’re looking for that next new wave. The adrenaline-pumping thrill of bungy jumping in Australia can only be topped by doing it while surrounded by stunning scenery.You will always feel safe and secure, as all Australia bungy jumping is run by certified professionals.

Rishikesh, India

Paragliding, rock climbing, trekking and river rafting can be best done here.

North is always exciting! The places, people, adventures, and vibes in the air make India’s northern frontier exciting. Rishikesh is one of the many places in North India that tends to every diehard adventurer. Among all the other adventures, trekking in Rishikesh is certainly one of the most exciting things to do. Meandering through those green meadows and walking those steep pathways one can find their way to the exciting destinations. These destinations in the foothills of the Himalayas are special in their own way.

Mount Titlis, Switzerland

Ice skiing, snowboarding and open cable car can be best done here.

With low-altitude mountains, sky-high peaks with near-vertical drops , powder-covered glaciers, and gently rolling Alpine forests, it's no wonder skiing and snowboarding in Switzerland tops most travelers' wish lists. Plus, the country is home to some of Europe's largest winter resorts, with new panoramic cable cars, high-speed chair lifts, and scenic trains offering easy connectivity between mountains.

Dubai, UAE

Parasailing, jet skiing and flyboarding can be best done here.

If you’re an aquaphile and absolutely love an adrenaline rush, the beaches in Dubai are exactly where you need to be heading to. Featuring nearly 50 km of coastline, make the most of your Dubai trip by stepping out to one of its many pristine beaches that support a variety of water sports. Whether you’re trying to beat the heat during the summers or just looking to tick a few things off your bucket-list, the beaches in Dubai promise a totally riveting and exhilarating experience. With well-informed guides to assist you in having fun, there’s clearly nothing that stops you from partaking in some thrilling water sports when you’re in Dubai.The Catamaran tour takes the water route to many of Dubai’s major landmarks like the Dubai Marina, JBR, Bluewaters Island, Atlantis, Burj Al Arab, Dubai Canal, Deira, Dubai Eye, Burj Khalifa, and Port Rashid and makes for a memorable experience. These landmarks are where all the water activtities happen with a beautiful view.
