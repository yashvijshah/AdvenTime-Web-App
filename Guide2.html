Fitness is the most important yet difficult part of an adventure. Some activities need a lot of muscle strength and leg strength. So, you need to make sure that you are fit enough to actually try it out. Read as much as you can about the adventure and start working on your diet and exercise regularly for atleast two weeks to get ready for the activity. Because otherwise you might not be able to do it or harm yourself in the process of trying.
Tools are very important to carry. Activities like trekking might need certain tools like rope, oxygen cylinders, and much more to go ahead of a certain point because otherwise it can be life threatning to continue. It is important to remember that challenging yourself is good but challenging yourself without required enquipments is stupid. Make sure you read what all you need before you start your experience.
Mental Health is as important as physical health. Some people tend to have anxiety or panic attacks and there isn't really anything that the instructor can do at that time. So either you get your medicine with you or you need to know when to stop. Make yourself mentally ready because if your mind is clear, you'll be able to enjoy a lot better.
Every adventure in life comes with a risk. As exciting as adventure sounds, these activities can be risky too if you don't follow the rules provided by the instructor. Adventure is fun but your life is at stake so you need to know about the risk that you are going to put yourself through before deciding to go for it.
